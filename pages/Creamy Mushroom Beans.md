- ## Ingredients
-
- 1 [[onion]], finely chopped
- 200g [[chestnut mushroom]], finely chopped
- 2 [[garlic]] cloves, finely chopped
- 45g [[tomato paste]]
- 15g [[dried mushroom]], soaked and finely chopped
- 70g [[cream cheese]]
- 120ml [[water]]
- 3–4 tbsp grated [[Parmesan cheese]]
- 1 400g can [[cannellini bean]], drained
- Chopped [[parsley]] (optional)
- 1–2 tbsp [[oil]]
- [[salt]], to taste
- ## Method
- 1. Soak the dried mushrooms in warm water for about 15 minutes (or longer if time permits). Carefully remove the mushrooms without disturbing the sediment, then finely chop them.
  2. Heat the oil in a large frying pan over medium heat. Add the onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the chestnut mushrooms and cook until all of their moisture has evaporated, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the garlic, tomato paste, and chopped dried mushrooms. Cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add the cream cheese, drained cannellini beans, and water. You may substitute some of the reserved mushroom soaking liquid for the water, avoiding any sediment from the bottom.
  6. Stir until the cream cheese has melted and the beans are heated through.
  7. Stir in the grated Parmesan and season with salt to taste.
  8. Finish with chopped parsley, if using, and serve immediately.
